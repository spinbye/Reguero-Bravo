In this scanned composition of 2024 I represent two
drawings I made with India ink and a crumpled glove
smeared in it, which I stamped on a white cardboard
and finished adding details with a fountain pen from
the shapes I saw. The bunny I later digitized by
drawing it on a tablet with the reference scan and
painted some areas gray. The petals of the flower,
as well as its square edges are related to the floor
paving tiles of Lisbon, that when some cobblestones
are missing,figures are formed on the floor.


The three roosters were made by experimenting in Illustrator
and Photoshop in 2021 where I modified the brush to give the
crest shape of the rooster and used two colors to distinguish
the eye from the top of the head. When I applied effects with
Photoshop it reminded me of a flower so it deserves to be next
to the one I have already commented.
