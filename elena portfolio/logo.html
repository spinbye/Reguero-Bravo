Reguero Bravo arises from finding the union of these
two apparently contrary words. A reguero is something
that is dropped, that comes out on its own. But a trail
can also be created by a brave horse when it gallops
strongly on the grass. I wanted to show as a flow with
a certain ferocity a selection of works that I have done
until now.

I asked my father to draw with his black Pilot the letters
that led you to this page, because he has the first surname
and good handwriting. I have modified them later with a
brush that I created in Illustrator because I liked that
they interpreted pixels as a glitch.
